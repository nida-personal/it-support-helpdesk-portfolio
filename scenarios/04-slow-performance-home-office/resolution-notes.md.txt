scheduled antivirus scan at 10am, large OneDrive sync, Teams, and Chrome
with 14 tabs open. Pausing OneDrive and closing browser tabs provided
immediate relief. Long-term fix: antivirus scan rescheduled to off-hours,
device flagged for RAM upgrade from 8GB to 16GB.
